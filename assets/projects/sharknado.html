Sharknado Animation

Sharknado Animation is written in C++ which originally started off as a model of a house that the user could rotate objects and change into different perspectives. With a growing understanding of the OpenGL Utility Toolkit (GLUT), there were many more objects and features added including zooming in and out, switching perspectives, moving the camera angle, and starting and stopping the animation.
